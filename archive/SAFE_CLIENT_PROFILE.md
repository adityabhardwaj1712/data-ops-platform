# Safe Client Profile (Sprint 5)

## Accepted Client Archetypes
- **Small Business / Founder / Agency**: Decision makers who are close to the problem.
- **Non-Urgent Needs**: The data is for analysis, enrichment, or a non-critical feature.
- **Flexible Timeline**: "I need this in about a week" is okay. "I need this by Friday 5PM" is NOT.
- **Outcome-Focused**: Wants *data* (CSV/JSON), not a dashboard, platform, or SaaS.
- **Technical Realism**: Understands that scraping is messy and not 100% perfect.
