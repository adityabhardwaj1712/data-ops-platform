# Internal Pricing Tiers (CONFIDENTIAL)

**PM Rule**: Pricing is protection, not greed.

## Tier 1: "The Happy Path"
- **Profile**: Static sites, clear pagination, structured HTML/JSON.
- **Effort**: < 1 hour manual setup/HITL.
- **Risk**: Low (Standard localized IP rotation is sufficient).
- **Minimum Price**: $X (Internal Baseline)
- **Acceptable Stress**: Zero.

## Tier 2: "The Moderate Lift"
- **Profile**: JavaScript rendering required, some messy data cleaning, specific headers needed.
- **Effort**: 1-3 hours manual setup/HITL.
- **Risk**: Medium (Requires monitoring).
- **Minimum Price**: 2.5x Tier 1
- **Acceptable Stress**: Low.

## Tier 3: "The Specialist Job"
- **Profile**: Anti-bot protections (simple), complex session management, very specific formatting needs.
- **Effort**: 3-5 hours manual setup/HITL.
- **Risk**: High (Potential for blocks).
- **Minimum Price**: 5x Tier 1 (Price for pain).
- **Acceptable Stress**: Moderate. Requires focus.

## Quote Calculator (Mental Check)
1. **Estimate HITL Hours** (Worst Case).
2. **Add 20% Buffer**.
3. **Multiply by Hourly Target**.
4. **Compare to Tier Minimum**. Take the higher number.
